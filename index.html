Empowering elderly artisans across India

Dori is a social-impact platform that connects elderly creators with customers who value authentic, handcrafted products. We help seniors earn sustainable income while preserving traditional crafts and dignity.

Mission: To create livelihood opportunities for India’s elderly by giving them a dignified platform to showcase and sell handmade goods.
Trusted partnerships with NGOs, old-age homes, and volunteers to onboard creators and provide tech support.

About Dori

Dori empowers elderly artisans (60+) by providing an online marketplace, technical support, and logistics so they can focus on what they do best — creating. Artisans receive fair payments directly after each sale, and Dori partners with NGOs and old-age homes to onboard and support seniors.

How Dori functions

1. Registration — Elder artisans (60+) register on the platform with help from family, volunteers or NGOs.
2. Product creation — Artisans make crochet bags, embroidery, home décor, baby wear and more.
3. Listing & marketing — Dori uploads product images and artisan stories, shares on social media.
4. Ordering & logistics — Dori handles packaging, shipping, and customer service.
5. Payment — Artisans receive transparent, direct payments after each sale.
6. Community building — Partnerships expand reach and add training opportunities.
