About Dori

Dori is a social-commerce platform that empowers elderly artisans (60+) by providing a marketplace, technical assistance for listing, and logistics support. We ensure transparent payments and tell the artisan's story alongside each product.

Marketplace
Curated listings — crochet, embroidery, knitting, woven goods and more.
Onboarding support
Volunteers and NGO partners help register artisans and upload listings.
Logistics
Packing, quality checks, and shipping handled by Dori.
Community
We amplify artisan stories and create local training opportunities.

How Dori works

1. Register — A caregiver, volunteer or NGO registers the artisan (we offer assisted registration).
2. Create listing — Upload photos, pricing and a short artisan story.
3. Sell & ship — Orders are processed; Dori handles packaging and shipping.
4. Payment — Transparent payments are released to artisans after sale.
5. Scale — Partnerships expand reach and training opportunities.
